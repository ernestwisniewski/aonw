# Code of Conduct

Age of New Worlds should be a welcoming project for players, contributors, and
maintainers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive, especially when giving feedback.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when your words or actions cause
  harm.
- Keep discussions focused on the project, its code, design, documentation, and
  community.
- Respect privacy. Do not publish private contact details, credentials, logs, or
  production data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, discussions, or maintainer
  decisions.
- Sharing another person's private information without permission.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately at `security@aonw.net`.
For security vulnerabilities, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, block accounts, or take other
reasonable action to protect the project community.
