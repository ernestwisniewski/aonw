Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Age of New Worlds handles data in the game, online services, and website.

Data stored on your device

The game stores saves, preferences, language settings, audio settings, performance settings, gameplay settings, and similar local data on your device.

Online accounts and multiplayer

If you create an online account or use multiplayer features, we process data needed to provide those features, including email address, display name, user identifier, authentication tokens, match identifiers, multiplayer commands, events, and match state.

Website and embedded media

The website may embed YouTube videos. If you play or interact with an embedded YouTube video, Google may process data according to Google's own privacy practices.

Advertising and sale of data

The current build does not use third-party advertising SDKs, and we do not sell personal data.

Security

Production network communication is intended to use HTTPS. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we keep data collection limited to what is needed to run the game and services.

Data deletion and contact

To request deletion of your online account data or other personal data associated with Age of New Worlds, email privacy@aonw.net with the subject line "Data deletion request". If possible, send the request from the email address connected to your account so we can verify ownership.

Data stored only on your device, such as local saves and preferences, can be removed by deleting the app data or uninstalling the game. Some limited records may be retained where needed for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or backups.
